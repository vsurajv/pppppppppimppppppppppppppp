IOT MCQ

1).What is the standard form of RREQs?
1.Route Requests
2.Route Reply Requests
3.Route Replies
4. None of the above
Ans. 1

2.) What is the standard form of RREPs?
1. Route Requests
2.Route Reply Requests
3.Route Replies
4. None of the above
Ans. 3

3).What is the standard form of RERR?
1.Route Error Requests
2.Route Error Replies
3.Route Error
4.None of the above
Ans. 3

3.)The RPL routing is used for _______ networks
1. Low power
2. Lossy
3.Both a and b
4.None of the above
Ans. 3

4).What is the standard form of LLN?
1. Low Lossy Network
2.Low Power Lossy Network
3. Lossy Network
4. None of the above
Ans. 2

5).The RPL protocol supports ______
1.Loop detection
2.Data path validation
3.Both a and b
4.None of the above
Ans. 3

6).The optimization objectives of routing includes _________
1.Minimizing the energy
2.Minimizing the latency
3.Satisfying the constraints
4.All of the above
Ans. 4

7).The operations of the protocol requires __________ links
1. Unidirectional
2. Bidirectional
3. Either uni-directional or bi-directional
4. None of the above
Ans. 2

8).What is the standard form of RFID?
1. Radio Frequency Identification
2.Radio Frequency-Independent
3. Radio Frequency-Dependent
4.None of the above
Ans. 1

9).The RFID tags consists of an _______
1. Antenna
2.Integrated circuit
3. Both a and b
4.None of the above
Ans. 3
